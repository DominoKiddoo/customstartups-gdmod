# Custom Startups
This mod lets you choose a <cy>custom video</c> that plays <cp>when the game starts</c>.
Add the <cr>netflix intro</c>, or even add the iconic <cb>valve</c> startup. There are endless possibilities for silly usecases.

## How to add:
To add a custom startup, first go to [this website](https://vidsplitter-pyapp.onrender.com/) which converts your video into an animated webp file, and an audio file <cr>(PLEASE BE PATIENT! HOSTING THIS FOR FREE MEANS VIDEOS CAN TAKE 1 MIN+ TO PROCESS. IF ENOUGH PEOPLE USE THIS MOD I MIGHT LOOK INTO PAID HOSTING)</c>. If for whatever reason the website is down (or you are impatient), you use [ezgif](https://ezgif.com/video-to-webp) to convert to webp, and [cloudconvert](https://cloudconvert.com/mp4-to-mp3) to convert it to mp3.
