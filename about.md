# Custom Startups
This mod lets you choose a <cy>custom video</c> that plays <cp>when the game starts</c>.
Add the <cr>Netflix intro</c>, or even add the iconic <cb>Valve</c> startup. There are endless possibilities for silly usecases.

## How to add a startup video
To add a <cl>custom startup</c>, first go to [this website](https://vidsplitter-pyapp.onrender.com/) which converts your video into an <co>animated webp file</c>, and an <co>audio file</c>. If for whatever reason the website is down/not working (or you are impatient), you use [Ezgif](https://ezgif.com/video-to-webp) to convert to <co>webp</c>, and [Cloudconvert](https://cloudconvert.com/mp4-to-mp3) to convert it to <co>mp3</c>.
